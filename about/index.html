About Yanlin Zhang

Yanlin Zhang

Applied Scientist at AWS Shanghai AI Lab
M.S. and B.S. from Fudan University

Introduction

I’m Yanlin Zhang, currently an Applied Scientist at the AWS Shanghai AI Lab. I completed both my Bachelor’s and Master’s degrees at Fudan University — with an undergrad focus in Optoelectronic Information Science, and a graduate degree from the Department of Electronic Engineering. During my graduate years, I worked with Professor Xiaodong Gu on knowledge graph-based recommendation systems.

Along the way, I’ve interned at Ctrip, Bilibili, and Amazon, where I had the chance to work on real-world recommendation challenges. At Amazon’s Graph Application Science team, I contributed to open-source projects like Tab2Graph and ELF-Gym, focusing on automated feature engineering powered by large language models. Our work was later published at CIKM 2024.

Right now, I’m especially interested in exploring:

  • How to design and evaluate intelligent agents

  • How large language models reason and make decisions
